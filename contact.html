FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often. Don't see yours? Send us a message above.

Do you accept PhilHealth?
Yes! BrightSmile Dental Clinic is a PhilHealth-accredited provider. Selected procedures like oral prophylaxis, extractions, and fillings may be covered. Bring your PhilHealth ID and our staff will assist you with the paperwork on the day of your visit.
How do I book an appointment?
You can book online through our Appointments page, call us at (054) 123-4567, message us on Facebook Messenger, or simply walk in during clinic hours. Same-week and same-day slots are often available.
Do you accept walk-in patients?
Yes, walk-ins are welcome Monday through Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We recommend booking ahead for longer procedures like braces or root canals to minimize waiting time.
Is the braces consultation really free?
Yes, the initial orthodontic consultation with Dr. Santos is completely free. He will assess your bite, take measurements, and recommend the best option — whether metal braces, ceramic, or clear aligners — with a clear price breakdown and no pressure.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, GCash, and Maya. For larger procedures like implants or braces, we offer installment arrangements. Ask our receptionist about our payment plans during your visit.
How far are you from Pili or Iriga?
We're located right in Naga City center along Magsaysay Ave. From Pili, we're about 10–15 minutes away. From Iriga City, around 30–40 minutes via the Pan-Philippine Highway. Ample parking is available near the clinic.
Do you treat children?
Absolutely. Dr. Ana Villanueva is our dedicated pediatric dentist and is wonderful with kids of all ages, from toddlers to teenagers. We make every effort to keep dental visits fun and stress-free for young patients.
Are all your dentists PRC licensed?
Yes. Every dentist at BrightSmile holds an active and current PRC (Professional Regulation Commission) license. You can view each dentist's credentials on our Team page. We also pursue continuing education to stay current with the latest techniques.

Ready to Visit Us?

Book your appointment online in just a few minutes — or drop by anytime during clinic hours. We'd love to see you.
