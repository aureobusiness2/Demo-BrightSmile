About Us

Trusted Dental Care in the Heart of Naga City

BrightSmile Dental Clinic has been serving families across Bicol since 2012. Our team of dedicated, PRC-licensed dentists combines modern technology with a warm, patient-first approach — because we believe going to the dentist should never feel stressful.

State-of-the-Art Equipment Digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, and laser-assisted procedures for accurate and comfortable treatment.
Patient-Centered Approach We take the time to explain every step so you feel confident and cared for at every visit.
PhilHealth Accredited Eligible procedures are covered under your PhilHealth benefits. We help you navigate your coverage.
